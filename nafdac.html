Simple 5-step guide to get registered

An overview â€” each product type (food, drug, cosmetics, medical device) has specific dossier rules. This hub points you to those official resources.

1. Create an account

Sign up on NAFDAC's e-Registration/NAPAMS portal and choose your client type.

2. Prepare your dossier

Gather product info, labeling, stability/quality docs and certificates as required for your product category.

3. Submit & pay fees

Upload your documents via the portal and pay the application fees as instructed.

4. Inspection & sampling

Where required, NAFDAC will schedule an inspection and may request product samples for analysis.

5. Review & registration

After evaluation and approval you will receive an official NAFDAC registration number and label approval.

Who is this for?

Manufacturers, importers, distributors and MSMEs who want to market food, drugs, cosmetics, chemicals or medical devices in Nigeria.
